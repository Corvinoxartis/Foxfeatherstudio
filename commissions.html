Commissions

This page outlines my commission pricing structure, including character framing, rendering, and background options.

General Information

All listed prices are for one character. For commissions with additional characters, each extra character adds 50% of the base price.

Example: if a base commission costs $20, then:

  • 1 character = $20
  • 2 characters = $30
  • 3 characters = $40

The base price of a commission is determined by the chosen character framing and rendering/coloring option. Background pricing is added separately based on the level of detail requested.

Turnaround Time

Standard commissions typically take 1–2 weeks to complete. More complex pieces—such as those with heavy rendering or detailed backgrounds—may take up to one month.

How to Commission Me

To inquire about a commission, please visit the contact page and include the following:

  • Character references
  • Desired framing and rendering level
  • Background preference
  • Any pose, expression, or scene details
  • Deadline, if applicable
